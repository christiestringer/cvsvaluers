About

Churton Valuation Services Limited (CVS) was founded in 2008 and is an independent valuation and property consultancy practice based in Auckland’s CBD.

CVS provides independent advice to a range of clients including private parties, a number of national and Australian companies, both listed and private, together with church and religious groups and other organisations.

Services

The services provided by CVS cover a wide range of purposes including:

  • Lease rental assessments,
  • Lease rental negotiations,
  • Arbitration and Expert Determinations – as an expert witness or where engaged as an Arbitrator,
  • Expert testimony at Court proceedings
  • Advice in respect of the sale and/or purchase of property,
  • For Mortgage lending purposes,
  • Asset portfolios valuations,
  • Replacement insurance assessments,
  • Valuations for Compensation purposes,
  • Matrimonial property,
  • Property consultancy

Churton Valuation Services Limited offers a high level of professional service that is timely, honest, accurate and cost competitive.



John Churton

The Principal, John Churton, is a Registered Valuer, who gained registration in 1984. He is a Fellow of the New Zealand Institute of Valuers and a Fellow of the Property Institute of New Zealand.

John has been involved in the valuation profession since 1984 and has been in private practice since 1989. He was previously a Partner and Director of Mahoney Gardner Churton Limited, then Gribble Churton Taylor Limited, before establishing Churton Valuation Services Limited in 2008.
